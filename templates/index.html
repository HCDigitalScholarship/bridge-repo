Welcome to The Bridge.

With this site, you can create customized vocabulary lists for ancient texts, popular core vocab lists, and textbooks. Focus on the vocabulary that matters to you: select all or part of the text you're studying, and exclude familiar vocab from what you've already read. Filter by parts of speech, frequency of occurence, and more. Download your lists and load them into your favorite flashcard app, or print them for use offline.
