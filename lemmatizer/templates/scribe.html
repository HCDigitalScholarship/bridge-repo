Bridge/Scribe will markup a text to reveal familiar and/or new vocabulary. For example, if you used the Bridge to create a list of new vocabulary in the first sentence of Caesar's Bellum Gallicum excluding the words in the DCC Latin Core, you would see:
Gallia est omnis divisa in partes tres, quarum unam incolunt Belgae, aliam Aquitani, tertiam qui ipsorum lingua Celtae, nostra Galli appellantur.
Currently in development; anticipated launch 2021.
